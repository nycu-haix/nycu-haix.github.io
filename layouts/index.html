About Me

I am Grace (Yu-Chun) Yen, Assistant Professor in Computer Science at NYCU. My work bridges HCI, AI, and social systems to support human learning, creativity, and decision making in real environments.

Our lab has evolved from "Creativity Lab" to "HAIX Lab" to reflect a broader scope: creativity, cognition, social computing, physical AI, healthcare, and game-based studies.

Current Focus

  • Human-AI Creativity Support
  • Cognition and Decision Intelligence
  • Social and Collaborative Computing
  • Physical AI and Healthcare Applications
