About Me

I am Grace (Yu-Chun) Yen, Assistant Professor in Computer Science at NYCU. My work bridges HCI, AI, and social systems to support learning, creativity, and decision making in real-world contexts.

Our lab has evolved from Creativity Lab to HAIX Lab to better match an expanding research scope and interdisciplinary collaborations.

Current Focus

  • Human-AI Creativity Support
  • Cognition and Decision Intelligence
  • Social and Collaborative Computing
  • Physical AI and Healthcare
