The Human AI & Creative Computing Lab is based out of the Department of Computer Science at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. We are reimagining human-AI and collaborative systems to empower people and improve society.

About Me

I am Grace (Yu-Chun) Yen, Assistant Professor in Computer Science at NYCU. My work bridges HCI, AI, and social systems to support learning, creativity, and decision making in real-world contexts.
